Project Case Study

Problem

The retail business relied on fragmented Excel reports and manual processes, causing delayed executive insights, inconsistent KPI definitions, and poor visibility into promotion effectiveness and inventory health.

Approach

Designed a centralized Power BI data model integrating sales, promotions, inventory, and store-level data. Implemented advanced DAX measures, optimized relationships, and built executive dashboards with KPI alignment.

Results

Delivered a scalable retail intelligence system providing real-time KPI tracking, promotion ROI analysis, and inventory performance monitoring — enabling faster executive decision-making and operational optimization.
